ABOUT

Est. 1978
Engineering
4 Institutions

Anna University was established on September 4th, 1978, as a unitary type of University. It offers higher education in Engineering, Technology, Architecture, and Applied Sciences. Besides promoting research, it fosters cooperation between the academic and industrial communities. It integrates four well-known technical institutions: CEG, ACT, MIT, and SAP.

Est. 1949
Rare Genius
Notable Alumni

In 1949, Shri C. Rajam established the Madras Institute of Technology. It was the rare genius and daring of its founder that made MIT offer courses like Aeronautical, Automobile, Electronics Engineering, and Instrument Technology for the first time in India. MIT has produced great scientists like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam and ISRO Chairman K. Sivan.

Golden Jubilee
Manufacturing
Robotics & Auto

The Department of Production Technology proudly celebrates its Golden Jubilee (1977-2027), marking 50 Years of Excellence. Established in 1977, it has grown into a leading multidisciplinary centre for Manufacturing, Automation, Robotics, and Mechatronics. It offers multiple prestigious B.E. and M.E. programs.

3rd Edition
Keynote Lectures
Global Network

The 3rd International Conference on Recent Innovations in Production Engineering (RIPE 2027) builds on a legacy of conferences in manufacturing, design, mechatronics, and automation. RIPE 2027 will unite researchers to exchange ideas, address challenges, and gain insights through keynote lectures by eminent speakers.
